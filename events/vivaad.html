Rules

  • TEAM SIZE: 1
  • The participants would be allowed to speak only in Hindi.
  • Two participants will be seated opposite to each other.
  • The list of topics will be displayed on the website ten days before the event.
  • The topics given will be social, political or religious on which a debate will be followed.
  • From the set of topics, every participant will be assigned one topic (for or against) 10 minutes before their turn. - Both the participants will be allowed to speak for 2 minutes.
  • At the end of the debate, the judges will ask questions to both the participants.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding on all the participants.
